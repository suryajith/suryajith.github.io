Brief Biography

I am an Assistant Professor with Center for Security, Theory and Algorithmic Research (CSTAR) at International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT Hyderabad).

My research broadly lies at the interface of Algorithms, Computational Complexity Theory and Coding Theory.

Previously, I was a post doctoral fellow at University of Haifa mentored by Prof. Noga Ron-Zewi, and at IIT Bombay mentored by Prof. Nutan Limaye. I got my MSc and PhD degrees in Computer Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI) under the supervision of Prof. Partha Mukhopadhyay. I got my undergraduate degree from IIT Kanpur.
